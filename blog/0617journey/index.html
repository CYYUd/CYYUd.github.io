0617 Journey in Europe - Königssee

By Dylan 29 October 2019
Königssee

The beautiful picture of lake remind me that there was a good old day i’ve enjoyed. I’ve traveled to Europe last Septemper just after i got my master degree in Institute of Electronics.

Königssee, located at southeast of Germany, known as the most claer and famous lake, formed by the glacier in the Ice age. The emerald water is even drinkable. We took the boat from Abfahrt der Boote to Salet and hike around the Obersee. Then went back to St. Bartholomä. The scenery along the way was fantastic, made me away from the disturbance in reality. It’s recommended to sit on the right side when taking the boat. You can see the “sleeping witch” and hear the “echo performance” because of the surrounding sheer rock walls. In addition, you can see the St. Bartholomä with its famous red, onion-shaped roof. Every single part in there is charming whether the scenery of mountains and lakes. It seems your mind were cleand in such the fairyland. Königssee is definately the must-go place in the rest of your life!
